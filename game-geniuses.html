Shigeru Miyamoto
Nintendo, Creator of Mario and Zelda

The father of modern gaming who created Super Mario, The Legend of Zelda, and many other Nintendo classics. His focus on fun gameplay over graphics revolutionized the industry.

Hidetaka Miyazaki
From Software, Creator of Dark Souls

Miyazaki-san reinvented difficulty in games with his Souls series (Dark Souls, Bloodborne, Elden Ring). His intricate world designs and punishing-but-fair gameplay created a new genre.

Hideo Kojima
Creator of Metal Gear Solid

The cinematic game director known for Metal Gear Solid and Death Stranding. His games blend complex stories with innovative gameplay mechanics.

Naughty Dog
Studio behind Crash Bandicoot and The Last of Us

This Sony studio evolved from fun platformers to creating narrative masterpieces like Uncharted and The Last of Us that rival Hollywood movies.

CD Projekt Red
Creators of The Witcher and Cyberpunk 2077

Started as a small Polish studio that grew to create The Witcher 3, one of the most acclaimed RPGs ever, before tackling Cyberpunk 2077.

Valve
Creators of Half-Life and Steam

Innovators who made Half-Life, Portal, and created the Steam platform that revolutionized PC gaming distribution.
