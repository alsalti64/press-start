This page explores the incredible history of home console systems, from their humble beginnings to the powerhouse machines of today.

PONG (1972)

One of the first computer games ever created, it's a very simple tennis game, yet it's seem that you can get addicted to it!

TETRIS (1984)

A timeless puzzle game where players arrange falling blocks to clear lines. Its simple yet addictive gameplay made it a global phenomenon.

Super Mario Bros. (1985)

A revolutionary platformer that laid the foundation for modern side-scrolling games and launched Mario into superstardom.

Sonic the Hedgehog (1991)

A high-speed platformer that introduced Sega's iconic blue mascot and defined '90s gaming cool.

Donkey Kong Country (1994)

Stunning pre-rendered graphics and tight platforming gameplay made this SNES classic a standout hit.

Super Mario 64 (1996)

A genre-defining 3D platformer that set the gold standard for 3D game design.

Metal Gear Solid (1998)

A cinematic stealth-action masterpiece that brought mature storytelling to video games.

Doom (1993)

The FPS that popularized the genre and introduced modding and multiplayer to a new generation.

Halo: Combat Evolved (2001)

Revolutionized console FPS gaming with tight controls, an epic sci-fi narrative, and LAN multiplayer.

Portal (2007)

A darkly funny and mind-bending puzzle game with the iconic portal gun mechanic.

Resident Evil 4 (2005)

Redefined the survival horror genre with action-oriented gameplay and over-the-shoulder perspective.

Bioshock (2007)

A haunting, philosophical shooter that blended immersive storytelling with rich world-building.

Dark Souls (2011)

A brutally challenging action RPG famous for its deep lore, punishing difficulty, and intricate level design.

Minecraft (2011)

An open-world sandbox that became one of the best-selling games of all time through creativity and survival.

Bloodborne (2015)

A Lovecraftian nightmare filled with fast-paced combat, haunting atmosphere, and deep lore.

Red Dead Redemption 2 (2018)

An emotionally powerful open-world Western epic with unmatched attention to detail and storytelling.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice (2019)

A tense and rewarding action-adventure set in a mythic Sengoku Japan, known for its precise swordplay.
