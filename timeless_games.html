PONG
1972

One of the first computer games ever created, it's a very simple tennis game, yet it's seem that you can get addicted to it!

TETRIS
1984

A timeless puzzle game where players arrange falling blocks to clear lines. Its simple yet addictive gameplay made it a global phenomenon.

Super Mario Bros.
1985

It's a platformer developed by Nintendo, it's the first game in the Super Mario series. This game was revolutionary in many ways.

Sonic the Hedgehog
1991

It's a platform game developed by Sonic Team, and marks the official debut of Sega's mascot.

Donkey Kong Country
1994

It's a platform game developed by Rare and published by Nintendo for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System.

Super Mario 64
1996

It is a platformer action-adventure game, and it is considered to be one of the most influential titles from the fifth generation of video games.

Metal Gear Solid
1998

A groundbreaking stealth-action game where players control Solid Snake, a special operative tasked with infiltrating a nuclear weapons facility.

Doom
1993

It is a 1993 first-person shooter, it pioneered and popularized the first-person shooter (FPS) genre, setting a standard for FPS games to come.

Halo: Combat Evolved
2001

Revolutionized first-person shooters with its immersive sci-fi universe, engaging story, and groundbreaking multiplayer.

Portal
2007

A mind-bending puzzle game that challenges players with its unique portal gun mechanic and darkly humorous story.

Resident Evil 4
2005

A groundbreaking survival horror game that redefined the genre with its over-the-shoulder camera and intense action.

Bioshock
2007

A masterpiece of storytelling and atmosphere that immerses players in the underwater city of Rapture.

Dark Souls
2011

A brutally challenging action RPG known for its intricate world design, deep lore, and rewarding gameplay.

Minecraft
2011

A sandbox game that lets players build, explore, and survive in a blocky, procedurally generated world.

Bloodborne
2015

A dark and atmospheric action RPG that combines fast-paced combat with gothic horror and intricate storytelling.

Red Dead Redemption 2
2018

An epic open-world western that immerses players in a richly detailed story of outlaws and redemption.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice
2019

A challenging action-adventure game that blends precise swordplay with stealth and a gripping feudal Japan setting.
