This website aims to educate people and encourage them to explore the history of video games by showcasing some of the iconic games, consoles, and the evolution of gaming culture. The website focuses on the different eras of gaming, additionally featuring visuals and descriptions of retro and modern video game consoles. And highlighting examples of games and their developers, some of the biggest names that have shaped the industry.

Our Objectives:

  1. Encouraging people to explore the history of video games.
  2. Showcasing iconic games and consoles.
  3. Comparing the different eras of gaming.
  4. Getting people to learn about the cultural impact of the gaming industry.
  5. Demonstrating the evolution of the gaming scene.
  6. Encouraging people to value the history of gaming for future generations.
